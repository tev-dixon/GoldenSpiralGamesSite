Game Hints

2's Trick

Determine if a number is divisible by powers of 2 by checking its last digits:

  • For 2: Check if the last digit is even.
  • For 4: Check if the last two digits are divisible by 4.
  • For 8: Check if the last three digits are divisible by 8.

Examples:

  • 1234 is divisible by 2 (last digit 4 is even)
  • 2512 is divisible by 4 (last two digits 12 are divisible by 4)
  • 5240 is divisible by 8 (last three digits 240 are divisible by 8)

3's Trick

A number is divisible by 3 if the sum of its digits is divisible by 3.

Example:

Is 423 divisible by 3?

  1. Sum the digits: 4 + 2 + 3 = 9
  2. 9 is divisible by 3

Therefore, 423 is divisible by 3

5's Trick

Determine if a number is divisible by powers of 5 by checking its last digits:

  • For 5: Check if the last digit is 0 or 5.
  • For 25: Check if the last two digits are 00, 25, 50, or 75.
  • For 125: Check if the last three digits are divisible by 125.

Examples:

  • 1035 is divisible by 5 (ends in 5)
  • 1350 is divisible by 25 (last two digits 50 are divisible by 25)
  • 3375 is divisible by 125 (last three digits 375 are divisible by 125)

7's Trick

This rule is more complex but faster than long division:

  1. Split the number into two parts:
    • All digits except the last one
    • The last digit
  2. Double the last digit
  3. Subtract this value from the first part
  4. If the result is divisible by 7, the original number is divisible by 7
  5. Repeat these steps on the result if it's too large to determine easily

Example:

Is 532 divisible by 7?

  1. Split into 53 and 2
  2. Double 2: 2 Ã— 2 = 4
  3. Subtract: 53 - 4 = 49
  4. 49 is divisible by 7

Therefore, 532 is divisible by 7

11's Trick

To determine if a number is divisible by 11:

  1. Starting from the leftmost digit, alternately add and subtract the digits
  2. If the final result is 0 or divisible by 11, the original number is divisible by 11

Example:

Is 4829 divisible by 11?

  1. 4 - 8 + 2 - 9 = -11
  2. -11 is divisible by 11

Therefore, 4829 is divisible by 11
